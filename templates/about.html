About

The main goal of the models is to predict whether the Bitcoin price will raise or not.

Models are ready to use, storing the historcal performance based on their predictions.

The implementation is flexible, allowing to add other models if needed.

Remember! We are talking about the prices at the beggining of the day!

Models

So far, three models are trained and implemented in the app:

RandomForest

AdaBoost

GradientBoost

They are retrained every time the server is started with the same hyperparameters.

You should consider looking at the performance tab, to decide whether they are trustworthy at the time.

Metrics

The performance tab comes with the historical and actual performance of the models. It contains metrics:

Precision - Percentage of correct growth prediction compared to the situations, where the stock price dropped, but the model said it will grow.

Recall - Percentage of missed growth opportunities.

There are more historical metrics stored, however these two are the most crucial, however there is always a place to implement them in the app.
